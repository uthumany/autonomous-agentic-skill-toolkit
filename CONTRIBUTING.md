# Contributing to Autonomous Agentic Skill Toolkit

We welcome contributions to this project! Please see our `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` for guidelines on respectful interaction.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork** the repository.
2.  **Clone** your forked repository to your local machine.
3.  Create a **new branch** for your feature or bug fix.
4.  Make your changes and **commit** them with clear, concise messages.
5.  **Push** your branch to your forked repository.
6.  Open a **Pull Request** to the `main` branch of the original repository.
